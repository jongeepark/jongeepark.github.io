About

I am a Professor in the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at Atilim University, Ankara, Türkiye. My research focuses on photocatalytic materials, dental and bio-ceramics, and first-principles (DFT) computational studies of energy materials such as battery anodes and perovskite solar cells.

I received my Ph.D. from Middle East Technical University (METU), and my M.S. and B.S. in Inorganic Materials Engineering from Hanyang University.

Research Interests

  • Photocatalysis — TiO2-based and doped photocatalysts, heterostructures for solar-driven hydrogen production and water purification.
  • Computational materials science (DFT) — 2D materials (TiC3) as anodes for Li-ion and Ca-ion batteries; perovskite solar cell materials.
  • Dental ceramics & bioceramics — apatite–wollastonite glass-ceramics, zirconia (YSZ), tribological and biological properties.
